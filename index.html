Anastasia Naidenova - the creator of unique interior paintings.
This is vivid minimalism with graphic elements described to have a unique and catchy look. A mixture of styles and techniques that add mood and meaning to the interior. Paintings and murals for the decoration of famous spaces in Russia and Canada, including the famous Jimmys Coffee chain in Toronto, the Molotoff Capsule Hotel in the old telegraph building in Moscow, and a special series for the PR Myasnitsky Boutique Hotel. Private orders Moscow, St. Petersburg, Tel Aviv, London.
